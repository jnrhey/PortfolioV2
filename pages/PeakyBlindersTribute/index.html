Thomas Shelby

"By order of the Peaky Blinders"

Biography

Thomas "Tommy" Shelby OBE DCM MM MP is the leader of the Birmingham criminal gang Peaky Blinders and the patriarch of the Shelby Family.

Born

1890, Small Heath, Birmingham

Occupation

Gang Leader, Businessman, MP

Military Awards

Distinguished Conduct Medal (DCM)
Military Medal (MM)
World War I Veteran

Civilian Honors

Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE)
Awarded for distinguished service to British industry and the expansion of exports

Arthur Shelby Jr.

The Eldest Shelby Brother

Arthur is the eldest of the Shelby siblings and serves as the Chairman of Shelby Company Limited. Despite his position, he often struggles with his violent tendencies and mental health issues.

Key Information:
  • Position: Chairman of Shelby Company Limited
  • Role: Head of Security
  • Gang Status: Senior Member of Peaky Blinders
  • Military Service: Former Sergeant Major
John Shelby

The Third Shelby Brother

John is the third eldest Shelby sibling and one of the most loyal members of the Peaky Blinders. Known for his quick temper and skilled fighting abilities, he plays a crucial role in the family business.

Key Information:
  • Position: Company Secretary
  • Gang Status: Third in Command
  • Military Service: Former Army Private
  • Family: Married to Esme Lee
Ada Shelby

The Shelby Sister

Ada is the only female Shelby sibling and initially distances herself from the family business. However, she later becomes an integral part of the company's expansion, especially in the United States.

Key Information:
  • Position: Head of Property and Acquisitions
  • Political Views: Former Communist
  • Residence: London, Later Boston
  • Family: Widow of Freddie Thorne
Polly Gray

The Matriarch

Elizabeth "Polly" Gray is the matriarch of the Shelby family and the treasurer of Shelby Company Limited. She ran the family business while the boys were away at war and continues to be a crucial advisor to Thomas.

Key Information:
  • Position: Company Treasurer & Chief Financial Advisor
  • Family Role: Aunt to the Shelby siblings
  • Special Abilities: Clairvoyant abilities
  • Notable Achievement: Ran the business during WWI
Grace Shelby

Thomas's First Wife

Grace Burgess Shelby initially worked as an undercover agent for the Crown but fell in love with Thomas. Their relationship evolved from enemies to lovers, eventually leading to marriage.

Key Information:
  • Background: Former Crown Agent
  • Marriage: Thomas's first wife
  • Children: Mother to Charles Shelby
  • Death: Assassinated in Series 3 by Italian mafia
Lizzie Stark

Thomas's Second Wife

Lizzie's journey from a former prostitute to Thomas Shelby's wife and a respected member of the Shelby family showcases her resilience and adaptability. She becomes an essential part of both the family and the business.

Key Information:
  • Position: Former Company Secretary
  • Status: Lady of Arrow House
  • Children: Mother to Ruby Shelby
  • Character Development: From secretary to lady of the manor
Duke Shelby

Thomas's First Born Son

Duke Shelby is Thomas's illegitimate son from a wartime romance in 1916. He was discovered during the events of Season 6, bringing a new dynamic to the Shelby family.

Key Information:
  • Status: First Born Son of Thomas Shelby
  • Background: Born to a Gypsy mother during WWI
  • Introduction: Season 6
  • Skills: Natural aptitude for the family business
Charles Shelby

Son of Thomas and Grace

Charles Shelby is the son of Thomas and his first wife Grace. Born into wealth and privilege, he represents the legitimate heir to the Shelby empire.

Key Information:
  • Parents: Thomas Shelby and Grace Shelby
  • Status: Second Born Son
  • Residence: Arrow House
  • Education: Privileged upbringing
Ruby Shelby

Daughter of Thomas and Lizzie

Ruby is the daughter of Thomas and his second wife Lizzie. She inherited some of her father's Romani heritage and showed signs of having special abilities. She tragically passed away from tuberculosis in Series 6.

Key Information:
  • Parents: Thomas Shelby and Lizzie Stark
  • Status: Deceased (Series 6, tuberculosis)
  • Heritage: Part Romani
  • Special Traits: Showed signs of second sight
